about

system description

I work on the layer most people never see: the services that move data between clusters, route calls to the right agent, and keep retrying quietly when the network doesn't cooperate. 2.5+ years writing async Rust on Tokio and Axum, with Go, Python and Node.js as supporting tools depending on what the system needs.

Most of my work lives in distributed data infrastructure — adapters over TiKV, Couchbase, Kafka, Elasticsearch and MinIO; replication pipelines that move records cluster-to-cluster with strong consistency; and event-driven services that have to stay correct even when something downstream fails.
